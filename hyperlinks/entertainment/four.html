Xian Lim thrives as director

Actor and newbie filmmaker Xian Lim's excitement bounced off the Zoom screen during the media conference for the second film he completed from the director's chair. A fantasy comedy titled "Hello, Universe!" his giddy energy made more and more sense as he eagerly chatted away about the upcoming Vivamax Original release.

First of all, Lim's follow-up to his first directorial project, "Tabon" in 2019, is likewise his screenplay and a story that is very close to his heart. "If you will remember, the main goal as to why I moved from the States to the Philippines was to become a basketball player. I remember na wala akong iniisip na plan B, plan C. Yun lang talaga ang dream ko," he started to explain. "Pero, life has a way of steering you to another direction, di'ba? Hindi kaagad ibibigay ng buhay sa'yo kung ano 'yung hiningi mo but it eventually led me to a better path in showbiz."

Grateful to Gibbs and the rest of the cast for appreciating and helping him bring to life his "dream movie," Lim will definitely thrive even more behind the camera.

"Dito po lahat naka-focus sa 'Hello, Universe' ang puso at kaluluwa ko," he finally said. "And I can't wait for everyone to watch it."

Hello, Universe also stars Anjo Yllana, Maui Taylor, Sunshine Guimary and Gene Padilla. The streaming date on Vivamax is yet to be announced.
