Nicky Youre shines bright with 'Sunroof'

American singer-songwriter Nicky Youre is making a name for himself via his now viral single "Sunroof."

"I'll leave the actual meaning to personal interpretation but for me it's really just a song that is supposed to be easy to listen to and something you can toss on at any moment without it feeling weird or out of place. That was the reason I wanted to make it," Youre said in a Zoom interview with Music Geek

"I would say it's supposed to be something that is lighthearted and remind you to just go enjoy life, do your thing, don't overthink and just have a good time with it," the artist added. poster.Now signed under Columbia Records, he collaborated with Dazy on this track.
