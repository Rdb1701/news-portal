'Lyric and Beat' celebrates Jonathan Manalo's musicality

Jonathan Manalo, the — prolific and multi-awarded songwriter, composer, arranger and record producer — is one of the most respected and celebrated music personalities in the Philippines today.

A recipient of the inaugural SUDI National Music Award from the National Commission for Culture and the Arts in 2021, he first emerged in the local music scene when he won 2001's "JAM: Himig Handog sa Makabagong Kabataan" for the now-iconic song "Tara Tena."

Today, his decorated career includes 75 multi-platinum and 100 gold Philippine Association of the Record Industry (PARI) certifications. He is also the recipient of more than 200 awards and nominations from several prestigious local and international award-giving bodies and is the creative mind behind the key albums and full discographies of OPM's most successful artists. His notable pop hits include "Pinoy Big Brother's" emblematic theme song "Pinoy Ako," Gary Valenciano's "Ililigtas Ka Niya," Aiza Seguerra's "Para Lang Sa 'Yo," Piolo Pascual and Sarah Geronimo's "Paano Ba Ang Magmahal."

And so, to celebrate his two-decade discography, his compositions are given a refreshing treatment in "Lyric and Beat," the first original light musical drama series of iWantTFC.
