A 'Gray Man' and 'Super-Pets'

Last weekend, I sat at the press screening for "DC League of Super-Pets" with some hardcore DC fans and families with kids â€” what I figured would be the obvious target market for this film. Yet I may have had as much fun as they did.

This movie got me because I'm a proud and happy pet owner of a fluffy black mixed-breed dog that sometimes looks like a little fox, a little like a wolf, an otter or a bear.

So from the time Krypto wrests and wiggles himself free from Jor-el's arms to jump straight into the Kryptonian pod that sends little Kal-el to Earth, I knew this film was going to pull all the strings and push all the buttons to exploit all the sweet things one feels from a pet and human bond.

The script actually got me invested. Will super dog Krypto be able to save his human, Superman and his Justice League teammates, who have all been knocked out and kidnapped? Does his new team of scrappy shelter animal friends led by Ace (Kevin Hart) actually have what it takes to pull this off?
