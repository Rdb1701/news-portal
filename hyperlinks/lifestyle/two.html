Celebrate Spongebob's birthday with 'Bikini Bottom Bash'

Kids are in for a nautical party this July as Sky celebrates SpongeBob SquarePants' birthday, filled with prize giveaways and back-to-back episodes of beloved SpongeBob show in the "Bikini Bottom Bash" every weekday until July 29, 5 p.m. on Nickelodeon.

Along with brand new episodes of "SpongeBob SquarePants" and its spin-off shows "Kamp Koral: SpongeBob's Under Years" and "The Patrick Star Show," there will be giveaways of exclusive SpongeBob goodies from Nickelodeon on Sky's official social media pages. Plus, wrapping up the surprises is a special birthday treat from sought-after Kapamilya P-Pop group BGYO, joining the party with a fun-filled performance posted on Nickelodeon Asia's Facebook and Instagram accounts.
